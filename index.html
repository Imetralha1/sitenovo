Meeple

Meeple is an epic brawler, of the control class. He knows the rules to every board game in existence and if you break them. .. Well let's just say you don't want to break them. They hate cheaters and those who don't follow the rules. There's one exception to the rules... Meeple themselves!

GADJETS

Mansions of Meeple

Meeple's next attack creates dice towers around the impact area and deals 500 damage.


Ragequit

Meeple lashes out in rage at nearby enemies, stunning them and knocking them back. Ragequit stuns for a longer period of time the fewer hitpoints Meeple has.

STAR POWERS

Do Not Pass Go

When you shoot through environment, deal 300 more damage.


Rule Bending

Critical success also increases allies' reload speed within the area by 20%.
